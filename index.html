About the Artist

Handmade collage, rooted in Nebraska

Liz Plettner works in mixed media collage, piecing together paper, pattern, and color into compositions that reward a closer look. Each piece is built by hand — layer by layer — until the balance feels just right.

Her work draws on texture and juxtaposition, turning everyday paper into something unexpected and personal. New pieces, works in progress, and behind-the-scenes studio moments are shared regularly on Instagram.
